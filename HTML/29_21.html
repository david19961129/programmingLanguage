The vertical-align Property

vertical-align: baseline (default):

An baseline image with a default alignment.

vertical-align: text-top:

An text-top: image with a text-top alignment.

vertical-align: text-bottom:

An text-bottom image with a text-bottom alignment.

vertical-align: sub:

An sub image with a sub alignment.

vertical-align: sup:

An super image with a super alignment.

An baseline image with a super alignment.

An text-top: image with a super alignment.

An text-bottom image with a super alignment.
